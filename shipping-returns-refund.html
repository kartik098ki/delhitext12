Shipping, Returns & Refund Policy

Last updated: 2025

This policy outlines the guidelines for shipping, returns, and refunds for purchases made on https://bejewelled-bonbon-30b7b1.netlify.app/.

1. Shipping Policy

A. Processing Time

All orders are processed and prepared for shipment within 1 to 3 business days (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays) after the order is successfully placed.

B. Shipping Time & Delivery

Standard delivery time is typically 5 to 10 business days from the date the order is shipped.

Delivery times are estimates and are not guaranteed. Delays may occur due to shipping carriers, customs processing (if applicable), or unforeseen circumstances.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email confirmation with tracking details.

C. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the size or weight of your order.

Free shipping may be offered on orders above a specified value, which will be clearly mentioned on the Website.

D. Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is marked as delivered but not received, please contact the shipping carrier first.

If your item arrives damaged, contact us within 24 hours of delivery with your order number and clear photos of the damage.

2. Returns & Refund Policy

A. 7-Day Return Window

Most items can be returned within 7 days from the date of delivery.

B. Eligibility for Return

  • The item must be unused, unworn, and in original condition
  • Original packaging, tags, and labels must be intact
  • Items must not show signs of wear, washing, or alteration

C. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return unless damaged or defective:

  • Custom or personalized items
  • Final sale or clearance items

D. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return, please email us within 7 days of delivery at: kartikguleria12@gmail.com

  • Include your Order Number
  • Mention the reason for return
  • Wait for return approval and instructions

Do not send items back without authorization. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.

E. Refund Processing

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you via email regarding approval or rejection.

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days.

Exchanges or replacements will be shipped within 5 to 7 business days.

Shipping charges are non-refundable. If a refund is issued, original shipping costs will be deducted.
